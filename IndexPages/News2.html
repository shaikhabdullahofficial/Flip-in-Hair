"HONEY, JUST SHAMPOO ISN'T ENOUGH!" - A BLOG POST FOR MEN
"HONEY, JUST SHAMPOO ISN'T ENOUGH!"

Have you ever heard this sentence from your loved one? We women are, at least as far as our hair is concerned, true pamperers. We cream, spray and distribute with great enthusiasm all kinds of things into our hair. Shampoo, hair treatment, conditioner, leave-in care, styling sprays and so on pile up on our bathroom shelves and take care of what (ideally) is then so beautifully silky shiny, nobly scented and wonderfully swinging, being a feast for the eyes of every observer. So, trust your woman when she says that even male hair needs some care and attention. It doesn't have to be the "overnight care package", for which one - equipped with a plastic shower cap - cannot sleep properly all night long because one is afraid of completely smearing up the pillow by rolling around comfortably in one's sleep. The fact that it could be covered freshly anyway is unfortunately irrelevant. But for this one would have finally used this "Pension Sonnenhof" plastic shower cap, which was taken home from the last vacation in Austria and which since then has been negatively noticeable in the bathroom drawer through unnecessary space consumption and senseless hanging around.

The good news is that it does not always have to be a huge effort. It can also be easier, with a conditioner. A shampoo cleanses the hair of styling leftovers and dead skin cells and removes excess sebum from the scalp. This will necessarily roughen the cuticle layer of the hair. This is where a conditioner comes in. Through the opened outer layer of the individual hairs, the conditioner's contained care substances can enter deeply and unfold their effect. Especially with certain "hair aches" such as hair loss, thinning or dry hair, an additional portion of nutrients and care can make a significant difference. The hair also becomes more resistant to damaging of environmental influences. The conditioner, which is massaged in after washing the hair and rinsed out again after a short period of a time, additionally ensures that the individual cuticles of the hair surface close up properly. So after washing, the hair remains better cared for, stronger and shinier, and can be styled better. And the effort involved is really limited.
