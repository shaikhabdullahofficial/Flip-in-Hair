GHD HELIOS® – THE INNOVATION FOR FULL STYLING CONTROL

The ghd helios® Hairdryer is the latest addition to ghd. The ghd brand is well known for its high quality and efficient electrical appliances and the new hairdryer has also quickly convinced us. The ghd helios® represents a revolution and now defines the fastest, lightest and most accurate hairdryer in the ghd family. As usual, we will introduce this innovation in more detail.

THE TECHNOLOGY OF THE GHD HELIOS

The ghd helios® offers the customer FASTER AND MORE POWERFUL DRYING - the maximised blow-drying power with the 2200 watt motor makes this possible.
A wave-shaped heating element and special nozzle provide a concentrated airflow at 120km/h. The brushless motor makes the hair dryer more durable.
The new generation of hair dryer comes around the corner with PRECISE STYLING CONTROL - The new design ensures even weight distribution for easy handling and effortless styling. The hairdryer weighs only gentle 520g without nozzle and cable.
The AEROPRECIS™ TECHNOLOGY combines the aerodynamic design inside with the precisely fitting precision nozzle. This not only concentrates the airflow, but also the temperature distribution. This ensures fast and perfect styling and an optimal fixation of the hair and the look.
What exactly is the Aeroprecis™ technology? This unique technology is a combination of four elements that combine to make ghd helios® so revolutionary.
The FILTER - The grille design helps to direct air efficiently into the filter and the special design with holes in the filter ensures an even airflow and maximum air intake.

The ROTOR is designed to take advantage of aerodynamics and reduces air turbulence and the associated noise level.

The STATOR VANES convert the air vortex into a concentrated air flow, thus ensuring a faster drying time.

The NOZZLE has a very narrow oval shape which allows the air to flow through the entire width of the nozzle. Swirls are reduced.

The advanced ion technology ensures BRILLIANT RESULTS. A flawless, shiny and smooth finish is created. The different temperature and speed levels allow full control over the styling and the separate cold button easily fixes the look and adds an extra dose of shine. Once you have chosen the ghd helios® hairdryer you can choose from four colours. Whether black, bordeaux, dark blue or white - the choice is yours. The hairdryer is covered with silver or gold detailing depending on the base colour. A suspension loop made of silicone serves for tidy storage.

THE INNOVATION IN THE TEST

We have of course tested the ghd helios® for you. I don't want to anticipate too much, but this lightning-fast hairdryer solves so many problems.

Every woman with long or thick hair knows the following problem: washing and grooming your hair doesn't take that much time. But the subsequent drying and styling of the hair can be lengthy and tedious. Here comes the first highlight of ghd helios® - the drying time. I normally need about 8 minutes to blow dry my hair. With ghd helios® this time is reduced by 50% to just 4 minutes. This is not only a huge time saving, but also simplifies hair washing. To dry my hair quickly, I blow dry my hair without the nozzle attachment. To get a precise styling, I use the nozzle. I find the noise level on the ghd helios® very pleasant. The hairdryer is exceptionally quiet, making styling very comfortable.

My hair is very susceptible to frizz and I often have the problem that it sticks out after drying. However, after drying with the ghd helios® there is no trace of flying or sticking hair. On the contrary, the hair is very shiny and feels smooth and soft. The overall appearance of the hair has changed. And although the hair falls very softly, it has a strong volume and stand. With a large round brush you can easily style beautiful waves. If curls don't last long with you, you can use the cold button here. Simply turn the hair on the round brush, blow-dry it, leave it on the brush and blow-dry it cold with the cold button. This is how you fix the waves in your hair.

CONCLUSION

In summary - the ghd helios® is a combination of tremendous power and precise control that turns your styling routine upside down and accelerates extremely.
